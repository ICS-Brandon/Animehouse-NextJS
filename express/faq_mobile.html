Anime House

A place where I post my thoughts and reviews of various Anime

"Why Was This Website Created?"

To make a long story short it's cause I have literally nothing better to do than build stuff, so I did. In actuality though, it was becuase I felt my university education in computer science was severely lacking for what is to be expected and assumed from a fresh graduate. I could have gone in dark into my career but I didn't want to be screwed from the start, so I began a search for projects to undertake. Website development has been my largest known weakpoint for a while now so I decided to get nice and personal with them to learn better. I also really like anime and always have so much to talk about with each episode so it made sense to combine the two so I don't get bored and to make sure I'm putting forward a solid effort.

"Who's This Website For?"

It's for anyone and everyone that enjoys anime and likes to hear about someone elses thoughts on it. The end goal is to create something that people are interested in visiting to get opinions, gush about an episode, and those sorts of things. The content that will be covered on here can definitely be explicit at times (violence, gore, etc.), so be warned for those things. There will not be, however, any explicitly sexual content covered on here. It might not be hentai but there are quite a few anime that definitely cross that line.

"What's Your Favorite Anime?"

There is literally zero correct answer to that question. Hell, I don't even have an answer to begin with. That's part of the reason that I'm doing this, I don't have a singular favorite anime, there's just too many with varying types, animation, voice actors, art, stories. There's just too much of everything to be able to narrow it down to a single pick. Plus, I feel, when you find that quintessential show the fun is over. Once you've found the best you're admitting that nothing else can top it, nothing else could change your mind. So as long as I'm still interested in anime I definitely won't have a singular favorite anime, I'll have one hell of a watch list to recommend though.
